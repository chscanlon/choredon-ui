Neutral ramp

An 11-stop warm-neutral ramp used by the Flux and Filament adapters to replace Tailwind's zinc. Anchored by Vellum at the light end and ink-near-black at the dark end. Every non-accent surface, border, and muted text in Flux and Filament reads through this ramp.

Sanity check: text on neutrals

Neutral 900 on Neutral 50 — body text on the lightest surface.

Neutral 800 on Neutral 100 — primary text on the Vellum page.

Neutral 600 on Neutral 100 — tertiary metadata on Vellum.

Neutral 100 on Neutral 900 — inverse surface, for dark sections.
